# Archive

Anything you delete — classrooms, sessions, assignments, announcements, reports, payments — goes to the **Archive** first instead of being permanently removed. This is a safety net so you don't lose data by accident.

## Restoring deleted items

```mockup
archive-panel
```

The Archive lists every deleted item across the academy in one place so you can recover anything someone removed by mistake. Restoring returns the item to its original tab with every linked record — students, attachments, scores — exactly as it was before deletion.

1. Go to **Archive**.
2. Find the item you want back.
3. Click **Restore**. It returns to its original location with all its data intact.

## Permanently deleting

If you genuinely don't want something back:

- Delete an individual item from the Archive — it's gone for good.
- Click **Delete All** to empty the entire Archive at once.

> ⚠️ Permanent deletion is irreversible. Once an item is removed from the Archive, it cannot be recovered.
