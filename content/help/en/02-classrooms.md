# Classrooms

A classroom is one of your classes — e.g. "Mars" for 4th-grade English. Sessions, assignments, attendance, and report cards all hang off a classroom.

## Creating a classroom

1. On the left panel, click **Classrooms**.
2. Click **Create a Classroom** at the top-right corner.
3. Fill in the form that pops up:
   - **Classroom Name**, **Grade**, **Subject**, **Teacher**
   - **Color** — for your convenience; helps you spot the class at a glance.
   - **Class Schedule** — pick the day(s) and time. Once set, sessions for the whole year are auto-created.
   - **Student Enrollment** — add students. *Note: students must already be registered in the system and linked to a family before you can add them here.*
   - **Notes** — any reminders or details about the class.
4. Fields marked with `*` are required.
5. Click **Create Classroom**. The new classroom appears in the Classroom tab.

## Editing or deleting a classroom

From the Classroom tab, open the class and use the edit or delete buttons. Deleted classrooms go to **Archive** first — you can restore them if it was a mistake.
