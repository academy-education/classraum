# Contacts & families

The Contacts section is your directory of everyone connected to the academy — managers, teachers, parents, students. What you see depends on your role.

## Who sees what

**Owners** see Managers, Teachers, Families, Parents, and Students.

**Managers** see Teachers, Families, Parents, and Students (but not other managers).

**Teachers** see Families, Parents, and Students (not managers or owners).

## Managers (owners only)

Visible only to owners. The Owner is the only role with higher privileges than a Manager. Owners can disable manager accounts — useful when a manager leaves the academy.

Both managers and owners share access to the finance tab and the ability to disable accounts.

## Teachers

Teachers can access only teaching-related features:

- Their own sessions, assignments, and attendance
- Not management, finances, or other teachers' classes

Teachers can't edit, disable, or delete anything outside their own scope.

## Families

A Family links one or more parents to one or more students — typically one parent + one child, but the system supports siblings and two-parent households.

**When you create a family, the system generates two login links:**

- One for the parent(s)
- One for the student(s)

⚠️ **Send the correct link to each person.** If a student logs in using the parent's link, they may see a sibling's grades (if the sibling is also enrolled at the academy).

## Parents & students

Parents and students get the same basic features, with two differences:

**Parents** can view multiple children's grades and reports if more than one child is enrolled. Switch between children from **Settings → Select Student**.

**Students** can only view their own grades and account — no visibility into siblings.
