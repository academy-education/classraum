# Announcements

Send academy-wide notices to parents and students at once — schedule changes, holidays, important reminders, future planning.

## Creating an announcement

1. On the **Announcements** tab, click **New Announcement** in the top-right corner.
2. Enter the **Title** (e.g. *Summer Break Schedule 2026*).
3. Enter the **Content**.
4. **Select the classrooms** that should receive it:
   - **Select All** at the top of the list sends to every class
   - Or pick specific classrooms if the announcement is only relevant to some classes
5. **Attachments** — attach files in any format if you want to send documents along with the message.
6. Click **Create**.

When you click Create, the system automatically sends an in-app notification to everyone selected — including parents of those classrooms.

## Editing or deleting

Open the announcement from the list and use the edit or delete buttons. The announcement (and its notification trail) updates accordingly.
