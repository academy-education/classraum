# Messages & notifications

## Messages

Built-in 1-on-1 messaging between academy staff, teachers, students, and parents. Keeps all academy communication in one place — you don't need to use KakaoTalk or other third-party apps for school-related conversations.

**Sending a message:**

1. Click the message icon in the top-right corner of any page, or the **Messages** tab on the left.
2. Click **New Message** in the top-right corner.
3. Pick one recipient (managers, teachers, parents, or students registered to your academy). You can only message one person at a time.
4. Type your message and send.

Every time someone messages you, you get an in-app notification. To turn off message notifications, go to **Settings → Notification Settings**.

## Notifications

The Notifications tab is two things in one:

**Real-time alerts** when something relevant changes:

- A new assignment is created in a classroom you're in
- An announcement is posted to a classroom you're in
- Attendance is updated
- A new schedule or session is added
- Exam results are posted
- A report card is sent

**A notification history** — review past notifications you might have missed.

## Customizing what triggers a notification

In **Settings → Notification Settings**, toggle which event types you want to be notified about. Useful if you only care about new messages and don't want a buzz every time attendance is updated.
