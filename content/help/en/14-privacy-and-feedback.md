# Privacy & feedback

## Data retention

For privacy and compliance reasons, all account information — including deleted accounts — is securely stored for **1 year**.

## Permanent data deletion requests

If you don't want Classraum to retain your personal information after deleting your account, email **support@classraum.com** and we'll remove your data immediately. Depending on your role, processing may be instant.

## Role hierarchy

In order of privilege, from most to least:

1. Owner
2. Manager
3. Teacher
4. Parent
5. Student

## Account deactivation permissions

Only **owners and managers** can deactivate accounts. If a manager resigns, only the **owner** can deactivate that manager's account.

If your academy has multiple managers, they all share the same permissions and access — Classraum doesn't distinguish between senior and junior managers within the Manager role.

## Data visibility by role

**Owners, managers, and teachers** can all view family information (linked parent-student groups).

**Managers** have access to all classes, sessions, assignments, attendance, and report cards across the entire academy, including every teacher's report cards.

**Teachers** can only see and edit their own classes, sessions, assignments, and attendance. They cannot view other teachers' work or report cards.

## Sending feedback or feature requests

We read every suggestion. Email **support@classraum.com** with what you'd like to see improved. We can't promise to implement every request — development priorities vary — but every piece of feedback is reviewed.
