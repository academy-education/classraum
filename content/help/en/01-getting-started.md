# Getting started

Welcome to Classraum. This guide walks through what each part of the app does, then takes you step-by-step through the most common workflows.

## What each tab does

**Dashboard** — Available to managers and owners only. A single overview of your academy's revenue, today's sessions, active users, total classrooms, and more. Customizable: drag, resize, hide cards, or reset to default from the "Customize Dashboard" button.

**Classrooms** — Your classes. Name them however you like — by grade, theme, or number. *Example: if your 4th-grade class is named "Mars" and your 5th-grade class "Pluto," those become two classrooms.*

**Sessions** — Individual class meetings. *Example: if "Mars" meets Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, each day is a separate session.*

**Assignments** — Homework, projects, tests, quizzes, and similar work given to students.

**Attendance** — Record and view daily student attendance.

**Announcements** — Send academy-wide notices (holidays, schedule changes, important updates) to parents and students at once.

**Notifications** — Auto-notifies users when assignments, announcements, attendance, schedules, or exam results change. Also a history of past notifications.

**Messages** — Built-in messaging between academy staff, teachers, students, and parents. Keeps all academy communication in one place — no more juggling KakaoTalk and email.

**Exams & Scores** — Create, manage, and grade exams. Use for monthly progress checks, quizzes, midterms, finals, and placement tests.

**Reports** — Stores all report cards. Managers see every report card; teachers see only ones they created.

**Payments** — Owners and managers only. Create and send tuition notices to parents and students. Payments accepted online via credit card, KakaoPay, NaverPay, or Toss.

**Contacts** — Owners see managers, teachers, families, parents, and students. Managers see everything except other managers. Teachers see everything except managers and the owner.

**Archive** — Deleted items go here first (classrooms, sessions, assignments, etc.) so nothing is lost by accident. Restore or permanently delete from here.

**Settings** — Personal profile, language, theme, notification preferences, and academy branding (logo upload).

**Get Help** — Live chat with the Classraum support team.

**Upgrade Now** — Compare subscription plans and upgrade your membership.

## Where most people start

1. Create your **classrooms** (one per class you teach)
2. Add **students** and link them to their **families**
3. The system will automatically create **sessions** based on your class schedule
4. Take **attendance** and create **assignments** during or after each session
5. Send **report cards** at the end of each month

The rest of this guide covers each of those steps in detail.
