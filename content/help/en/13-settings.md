# Settings

Two sections in Settings:

- **General Settings** — available to everyone
- **Subscription Management** — managers and owners only

## General Settings

What you can change:

- **Personal information** — name, email, phone
- **Notification settings** — which event types trigger an in-app notification
- **Theme** — light or dark mode
- **Language** — Korean or English
- **Academy Branding** — upload your academy logo (replaces the Classraum logo in the app for your academy's users)
- **Account deletion** — permanently delete your account *(see [Privacy & data](/dashboard/help/privacy-data) for what happens to your data after deletion)*

### Uploading your academy logo

1. Open **Settings → Academy Branding**.
2. Upload your logo file. Recommended format: PNG with a transparent background.
3. The logo immediately replaces the Classraum logo in your academy's instance.

## Subscription Management (managers & owners)

- Change your payment method
- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- See how much storage you have left for the month
- Review the invoice history

You can also upgrade from the **Upgrade Now** button in the bottom-left of the sidebar — same destination, just a faster path.
